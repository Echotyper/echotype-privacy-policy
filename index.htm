EchoType Privacy Policy

Last updated: [09/06/2025]

1. Information We Collect

EchoType is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our typing challenge service:

  • Game Progress: Your typing scores, completion times, and progress through challenges
  • Display Name: A username you choose for leaderboards (optional)
  • Anonymous Analytics: Basic app usage statistics to improve the experience

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress and achievements
  • Display your scores on leaderboards (if you choose to participate)
  • Provide daily challenges and track streaks
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data remains private and is used only within the EchoType app.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is securely stored using Firebase services with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

5. Children's Privacy

EchoType is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of leaderboards and public features

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support section or via the App Store.
